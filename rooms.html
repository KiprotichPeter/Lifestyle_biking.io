Our Products

  • Stationary Bike

    With our stationary bicycle that creates electricity while you ride, you can get in shape while also helping the environment. Your pedaling is converted into electricity via the bike's generating system, which can then be utilized to power items or sold back to the grid. It's long-lasting, customizable, and ideal for anyone trying to make a difference in the world.

  • non-stationary bike

    Are you seeking for a fun and environmentally friendly way to explore your city? Consider our non-stationary bicycle, which can recharge itself as you ride! This bike is designed to transform the energy you make by pedaling into electrical energy, which can be used to power various devices or redeemed for cash.
