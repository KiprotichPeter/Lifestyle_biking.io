About

We Have Free Templates for Everyone

Welcome to Lifestyle Biking, where we have created two unique bikes, one stationary and one non-stationary, that can recharge themselves while you peddle. By delivering cutting-edge, environmentally friendly training equipment, we hope to encourage healthy living and sustainability.

Our stationary bike is designed to transform the energy you generate while pedalling into electrical energy that is stored in a rechargeable battery. The more energy you generate, the more energy the battery receives. Furthermore, our stationary bike has an interactive display that allows you to track your progress and earn incentives based on how much energy you produce.

Our non-stationary bike has similar features, but it also has the advantage of being portable. Because you can charge the battery while riding and utilise the stored energy to power the bike's electric motor, this bike is ideal for commuting or exploring new locations. The non-stationary bike also comes with a GPS tracker and a smartphone app that allows you to track your rides and development.

Our Community

Not only can our rechargeable bikes promote healthy living and sustainability, but they may also be utilized for commuting. You can lower your carbon footprint and contribute to a cleaner, healthier neighborhood by incorporating our bikes into your everyday journey.

Future Goals

At Lifesytle Biking, our future goals are to promote healthy living and sustainability on a larger scale. We intend to manufacture and distribute our rechargeable bikes throughout the United States, making them available to anyone who wants to improve their health and the environment.

We believe that out bikes,have the ability to revolutionise the fitness sector and provide a more sustainable future for future generations. We hope that by broadening our reach, we can encourage more people to live a better, more sustainable lifestyle and have a beneficial impact on the world around us.
